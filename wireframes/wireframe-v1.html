Meet Claude.

Not another chatbot. A thinking partner that asks better questions than it gives answers.

Full-width visual — Claude interface screenshot or animated demo showing a conversation where Claude asks diagnostic questions instead of just answering

Claude is built by Anthropic. It's not trying to be everything to everyone — it's designed to think carefully, push back when your question is wrong, and build context over time.

If ChatGPT is the employee who says "yes" to everything, Claude is the strategist who asks "have you considered this?"

The mindset shift: Claude isn't a search engine. It's not a content spinner. It's a thinking partner that gets smarter the more context you give it.

Over the next 90 minutes, you'll set up Claude, teach it who you are, and walk away with your first strategic document — built by AI that actually understands your business.

Getting Started

From zero to your first strategic document in 15 minutes.

Step 1: Create Your Account

Go to claude.ai and sign up. We recommend Claude Pro ($20 USD/month) — it gives you more messages, access to the best models, and Projects.

Screenshot of claude.ai signup page with arrows pointing to key elements

Step 2: Set Up Your First Project

Projects are Claude's superpower. They let you give Claude persistent context about your business — so every conversation starts with full knowledge of who you are, what you sell, and how you operate.

Screenshot showing how to create a new Project in Claude, with custom instructions panel highlighted

Step 3: Teach Claude Who You Are

This is where the magic happens. Instead of migrating your ChatGPT history (don't bother), you'll let Claude build a better picture of you from scratch.

The key insight: You don't need to bring your old conversations. Claude will build a better picture of you from scratch in 10 minutes than ChatGPT built in 6 months.

Start a conversation and let Claude ask you questions. It will search for information about your business online, then ask deeper clarifying questions to fill in the gaps.

Step 4: Build Your Foundation Documents

Head to the Prompt Vault (next tab) and start with the Business Profile. Each document builds on the last.

Flow diagram: Business Profile → ICP → Offer Doc → Brand Guidelines → Upload all to Project → Every future conversation starts with full context
